The Scene Party Faq

The What is and what should I bring to a Party Frequently Asked Questions and Answers. Shortly the Party FAQ.

Being a sysops who's inviting new scene people to parties I often get the same questions. Which I don't intend to answer for another hundred times..from now on i can simply refer to Imphobia ;-) Changes are marked with a |

Q: Can we go to the party if we don't enter the compo's ??

A: Yess of course... parties are not only meant to compete in the compos but also to have fun... meet some new friends .. talk about lot's of things in particular Code, Gfx and Music. You don't have to release anything at the party just be there and talk to as much people as you can.

Q: Have they computers for everybody there ?? ,Should I bring my own equipment ??

A: No they don't have computers for everybody there. You should bring your own equipment. Do not forget to bring your sound equipment also. And don't think it'll be damaged. My system has been in lot's of different countrys so far. From France over Belgium, The Netherlands, Germany to Denmark and Finland. Small note to sysop: leave yar BBS Home... It has to be Online.. bring some other computer !

Q: Where do I sleep ? Should I bring a sleeping bag ? Are there beds for everybody.

A: Depends on how big the party is. If it's a two day party don't bring anything ya won't sleep anyway... If it's a more day party bring your sleeping bag and something soft to ly on (girlfriend or something like that.. (that way we also see some girls on a party)) You will find a comfortable place in a separate hall somewhere.

Q: How much does it cost to come in ?

It depends on the party itselve. Big Parties cost usually around 30-40 USD and smaller partys will be half of that. But that includes electricity, warming and roof above your head for the whole weekend. Also the prizemoney is paid from that money. If you want to compare that to the prize you would pay to stay in a hotel for the same period it's simply cheap !

Q: Should we bring our own food ?

A: My anwer: YES ... lot's of parties have one big disadvantagement there it not enough food or the food is simply not good or too expensive. Unless the invitation form states you are not allowed to bring any bring as much as you think you can eat. At the Party I met some guys that had 12 boxes of ravioli with them (Zootje rules) Bring also enough bottles of Coke or other drinks. If you go to a party where you can't bring that much equipment (e.g. if you're going by train / plane) the first day will be your shopping day, go out to the local supermarkets (big cities have lot's of them..) and buy enough for the whole weekend.

Q: What happens if anyone steels our equipment.

A: It means you didn't pay attention enough. Make sure there always is somebody there who's looking after it.

Q: How do I get there ?

A: Actually there are 3 big posibillities I can advise. The First one is let you drive there. Let you drive 'cause when you drive back after having spent 60 hours without sleeping, you're a danger on the road. It's better to get somebody to drive you there. If you drive there yourselve make sure you slept enough before you leave back home. For the equipment you can put your cases in the back of the car and the monitors / amplifier / boxes in a trailer behind it. That way you can be 5 people in a normal car without sitting on a harddisk. Second posibillity is to go by train/plane. You can easily take your computer with you if you have a small cart. For planetrips take your case as hand luggage it's not too big. You may even ask the customs not to let it go through the scanner. Your monitor will be normal luggage. Third possibility join a bustrip somebody is organising to the party place (or organise on yourselve.) There will always be enough info when you look in the right places.

Q: What about showers and toilets ?

A: Some parties have the possibility to take a shower for a small price or even completly free. You might want to bring your towels and soap, please bring some clean t-shirt also. We don't want to smell you coming ;-) It might be usefull to bring some extra toilet paper also. Sometimes there isn't enough and to find some extra in the middle of the night is not that easy, off course you can always use your ms winslows manual for that problem also.

Q: How can we enter the competitions ?

A: At every well organised party you should be able to find an information desk. Normally you can fill in a form there and give your disk(s) with your contributions. I fyou can't find the information desk, try to catch one of the organisers and ask him. ... Actually you should always try to find a organiser you know a bit better than the others, if you have a problem it might always be usefull.

Q: I have 2 90x40x30 cm loudspeakers can I bring them ??

A: You are obliged to bring them, but you should use them at maximum volume at 03:00 some people might want to sleep. Bring some headphones also if you plan on finishing your tune. It's really annoying if you start composing at maximum volume. Oh yes... You'll have place enough to put them also.
