  1. How to get to the RAGE '96 Party place

    2. Kistarcsa - where the party will take place - is about 1km away far from the Budapest the Capital. It's between Ors vezer square and Godollo. Therefore you should take the HEV (suburban train) to get there. Get off at Kistarcsa stop (about 20 minutes). The distance between the school and the station is less than 500m. (See map). You could also take the bus 92 or if you come by car follow route 30 starts from Budapest. Parking place is reserved for visitors.

  2. Competitions

    3. We must ask anyone to include your works on 3.5" or 5.25" disks. We can not accept hard disks or other medias.

    All your products are must be freeware and the party organizers can spread it. So include all nfo and file_id.diz files cause we won't let anyone to modify their package after the competitions have finished.

    If you contribute a team work than one of the members must be available at during the party.

    None of the demos or intros can modify anything on our hard disk or any of our writeable media.

    Besides the music compo anyone can contribute more than one products. For example if you're a intro coder you can put more than just one 4kb intro into the "ring".
