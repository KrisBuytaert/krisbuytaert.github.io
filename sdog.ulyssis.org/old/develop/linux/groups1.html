Groups that are working on a Linux Production

  • Skal was the first one to release a Linux demo (as far as I know), at Wired'96 he won the pc demo compo and it was worth every piece of it. Mainly because the demo ran both with svgalib, X and dos ..
  • CRAP has lot's of members working on some linux stuff. So far they didn't show us anything yet. They seem to have a tracker and a pixeller ready and working on something else.
  • The Basque demo group IMD
  • The Natives are also working on their next demo, wether it'll ever get finished is another question, but if they release something new it will be a Linux product.
  • ArtworX enterred the Wired'97 linux compo, this is a group that has a great potential for the future , i hope to see a new production from them soon. They are working on a DPaint Clone at the moment and they hope to have the first release ready at the end of september.
  • New Order surprised everybody with their first demo ever, after making lot's of intro's and diskmags they moved to demo coding on the one and only platform. They came 3rd at Wired'97 after a night of wiskey and coding.
