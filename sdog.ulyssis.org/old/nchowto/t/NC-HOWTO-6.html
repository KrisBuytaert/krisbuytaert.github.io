TroubleShooting

Q: I get an Invalid Kernel Type while tryng to boot the NC.
A: You didn't run the agree script.
Q: I log in at the remote host and my keyboard settings fuck up.
A: I use no .Xmodap when working on the NC. This keeps my keyboardsettings perfect.

Experciences
